Our Story

Plan'N'Teach is a user-friendly and comprehensive Education Management System software solution that can address the needs of academic personnels which includes but not limited to: teachers, tutors, lecturers and students; in early preparation of lesson plans for their classes. The solution covers all arms of education including primary / secondary school subjects and university courses encompassing all topics and concepts under each subject/course.
